Project Description

This​ project was divided in two parts. Create a Digital game and a Board game.
Both this games have the goal of promoting the correct pratices to protect laurissilva, while at the same time giving the people some information about it.

BOARD GAME
Users play as 4 endemic species on a friendly race through the Laurissilva of Madeira, gathering friends along the way, learning about the forest’s significance, history, and their role in protecting their home.

DIGITAL GAME
The users have to take photos of endemic species, to be able to advance onto the next level, gaining more ​knowledge along the way, and discovering​ new and exciting places and animals.

Digital Game

Discover the magic of the Laurissilva Forest in the digital game memories of nature! Capture photos of native animals, learn fun facts about the flora and fauna, and explore exciting quests. View your photos at any time as you become an expert in this lush forest.

Board Game

Story Users Play as 4 endemic species on a friendly race through the Laurissilva of Madeira, gathering friends along the way, learning about the forest’s significance, history, and their role in protecting their home.

Objective The goal of the board game is to reach the end faster than your opponents while correctly answering trivia questions, adapting to random events and collecting species cards.
